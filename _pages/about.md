More recently, I've been researching into the field of **Computational neuroscience** with the [CoCo Lab](https://www.cocolaboratory.com/) at Weill Cornell Medicine.

In my free time, I'm an avid listener of rap/hiphop music and love to play tennis. I'm always looking for the next challenge, and am open to any opportunities to learn more about research, software development, and computer science in general!
