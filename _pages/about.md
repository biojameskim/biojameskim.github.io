Hello! I'm **James**, a senior studying Computer Science and Math at Cornell University. I'm captivated by the dynamic interplay between the human brain and AI—how the brain's remarkable adaptability and efficiency inspire new systems and algorithms, and how those developments, in turn, deepen our understanding of the brain. 🧠

My research thus far aims to strengthen this connection by building more accurate models of brain-behavior relationships, holistically integrating both the brain's functional and structural connections.

I've had the pleasure of conducting research with Professor **Amy Kuceyeski** in the [CoCo Lab](https://www.cocolaboratory.com/) at Weill Cornell Medicine. I also help out with organizing the [MELBA Symposium Series](https://www.melba-journal.org/symposium.html).

I'm also interested in how humans interface with technology and have explored the intersection of AI/ML, Ubiquitous Computing, and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) with the [SciFi Lab](https://scifilab.org) at Cornell.

In my free time, I'm an avid listener of rap/hiphop music and love to play tennis. I'm always looking for the next challenge, and am open to any opportunities to learn more about research, software development, and computer science in general!
